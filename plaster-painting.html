Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What age is this suitable for?

Our kits use safe, non-toxic paints. It is perfect for kids aged 3 and above. Younger kids enjoy bold coloring, while older kids love the name customization.

Q: How long does a custom order take?

Because every plaster piece is 100% handcrafted, poured, and dried meticulously, please place custom big orders 1-2 weeks in advance.

Q: Can the plaster be used for anything else?

Yes! Once fully dried, it works perfectly as an aroma diffuser stone. Simply drop a few drops of your favorite essential oil onto the back.
